Anyone But You Film A fun and lighthearted rom-com featuring
Sydney Sweeney and Glen Powell. While the film could
have gone deeper in the characters' personal turmoils,
it still makes for a great viewing start to finish! 		4.5/5
The Boy and The Heron Film Hayao Miyazaki has done it again.
From beautiful animation to spectacular world-building,
there is nothing about The Boy and The Heron
that wasn't executed with perfection.
Featuring a tender storyline embeded in a world of magical realism,
this movie is sure to keep you on the edge of your seat! 		5/5
Grief is the Thing with Feathers Book I always appreciate media that sheds light on
the grieving process, however I did find that a lot of
the messages within Grief is a Thing with Feathers
to have been lost amid all the stylistic implementations.
That being said, the ending of the book did manage
to bring it all neatly together in the end. 		3.5/5
My Name TV-Series If you're looking for a high-paced and action-packed tv-show,
look no further than My Name. Featuring a tough-as-nails,
morally-ambiguous heroine, My Name is sure to keep viewers engaged.
From beautifully choreographed fight scenes to
blood curdling mysteries and realizations, even the very last
minutes of the series will leave you gasping. 		5/5
Time Traveler's Wife Book An interesting take on the sci-fi/contemporary fusion genre.
While the story feels initially lighthearted,
you soon find yourself pulled into the mystery of the characters' situations.
Later on, the book also takes a more sinister turn, eventually leading
the reader on an emotional rollercoaster of highs and lows. 		5/5
